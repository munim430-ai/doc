How to use
1

Open the app

Double-click Folio 2.0.0.exe. No installation needed — it runs directly. Enter the password when prompted.

2

Download the student template

Click the "↓ Download student template (.xlsx)" link inside the app. Open it in Excel and fill in one row per student.

Full Name · Passport No · DOB · Gender · Nationality · Address · Sponsor info…
3

Upload & generate

Drag your filled .xlsx file into the app and click Generate Documents. One DOCX is produced per student row.

4

Download the forms

Download files individually or as a single ZIP. Forms are ready to print and submit to Mokwon University.

5

Updates are automatic

When a new version is available, the app will download it in the background and prompt you to restart. Nothing to do manually.
